About

The SNU Applied Econometrics Reading Group is a graduate-level initiative led by students from Seoul National University to delve into key econometric papers, explore cutting-edge methods, and foster discussions. Meetings are held every two weeks, focusing on a mix of theoretical and applied topics.

Significant efforts have been made to integrate Machine Learning and Deep Learning methods into the fields of economics and econometrics. Surveys on the economics applications of Machine Learning

  • Mullainathan and Spiess (2017)
  • Athey and Imbens (2019)

and Deep Learning:

  • Dell (2024)
  • Zheng, Xu and Xiao (2022)

Protocol

  1. Regular meetings will be held every two weeks.
  2. Two presentations per meeting, each consisting of a 30-minute presentation followed by a 15-minute Q&A session.
